Our Mission

Build community. Our mission is to proudly showcase our local residents’ talent by creating and building lasting community connections in Eufaula, AL. We aim to bring together and celebrate our artists and audiences through the magic of live theatre.

From the start is has been the theater's business to entertain people. It needs no other passport than fun.

Bertolt Brecht

Business Sponsorship Levels

Supporting Cast ($100) - Gratitude and acknowledgement on our Facebook page

Featured Player [Local Businesses ONLY] ($250-500) - Sly business or product placement with explicit mention during a single production. Video clip will be featured on our Facebook page. Must have a sense of humor! The reference will fit the context of the play (at the director’s discretion) and may or may not be flattering. (Example Promo: “Uneasy lies the head that wears the crown. But the head that wears a Eufaula Brand Fedora™ is always in style!”)

Lead Player ($500-$1,000) - Gratitude and acknowledgement on our Facebook page. Logo will be featured on our Facebook page as a “Lead Player Level” sponsor.

Director ($1,000-$2,499) - Gratitude and acknowledgement on our Facebook page and in the playbill for the duration of a single production. Logo will be featured on our Facebook page as a “Director Level” sponsor. Space to display signage (no larger than 5’x5’) at a single production.

Producer ($2,500-$4,999) - Gratitude and acknowledgement on our Facebook page and in the playbill for a full production year. Logo will be featured on our Facebook page as a “Producer Level” sponsor. Space to display signage (no larger than 5’x5’) for a full production year.

Executive ($5,000+) - Gratitude and acknowledgement on our Facebook page and in the playbill for a full production year. Logo will be featured on our Facebook page as an “Executive Level” sponsor. Mention during the curtain speech of every production for a full production year. Local Eufaula businesses at this level reserve the option to extend product placement mentions for five (5) production years.
